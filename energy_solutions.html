Comprehensive Testing and Compliance for Cells and Batteries

Cells and Batteries

November Services offers safety and performance testing of both primary and secondary cells and batteries for various kinds of applications. Testing is done for dry cells, button cells, lithium, nickel, and lead acid batteries, among others. Applications cover single-use throw-away batteries to rechargeable batteries for portable applications and solar inverters.

Regulatory Compliance

Labs are recognized by the Bureau of Indian Standards for testing cells and battery packs for regulatory compliance introduced by various Ministries, including MeitY (Ministry of Electronics and IT) and MNRE (Ministry of New and Renewable Resources).

Testing is also conducted for CE Marking and Consignment Inspection for goods targeted for exports. Test facilities are available for testing against various Buyers/Tender requirements.

Key Testing Facilities

Our labs are equipped with state-of-the-art facilities to perform the following tests:

Charging / Discharging Test
Insulation Resistance Test
Continuous Charging Test
Vibration Test
Moulded Case Stress Test
Temperature Cycling Test
Incorrect Installation Test
Crushing Test
External Short Circuit Test
Free Fall Test
Mechanical Shock Test
Thermal Abuse Test
